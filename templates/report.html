Copyright Information

DMCA Takedown Process

For copyright infringement claims, we follow the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) process:

1. Submit DMCA Notice

Send a formal DMCA takedown notice to DMCA@jarmod.com with all required information.

2. Review Process

We review the notice within 48-72 hours and take appropriate action if the claim is valid.

3. Content Removal

If the claim is valid, we remove or disable access to the infringing content promptly.

4. Counter-Notice

The content uploader may submit a counter-notice if they believe the claim is invalid.

DMCA Notice Requirements

  • Identification of the copyrighted work
  • Identification of the infringing material
  • Contact information of the complainant
  • Good faith statement
  • Accuracy statement under penalty of perjury
  • Physical or electronic signature
